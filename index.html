Local

With LOCAL, Lab Academia aims at bringing into the picture of Allahabad a medium through which one can gather accurate and precise information about the nooks and crannies of Allahabad. Local is not like your regular magazine or directory, here one can find the most basic information and at the same time be marveled at the never known facts about our cozy and affable town. Our team members are working day and night and putting their best foot forward to bring to you a foundation through which one can see Allahabad in a new and clearer light.
With LOCAL we endeavor to simplify the picture of Allahabad so that one can easily find what they are looking for without going through the hassle of searching a place on the internet, reaching there and realizing that the information was not authentic. LOCAL assures genuine and useful information.Besides being on print, LOCAL will be launched as a digital magazine as well, its authenticity making it stand out from others.
One can list with LOCAL for the best online as well as offline promotion.
